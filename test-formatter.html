  • Ice cubes

Phase 1: Opening Hook (10 minutes)

Begin by showing students an ice cube and asking what will happen when we heat it. Have them make predictions and record their thoughts.

  • Hold up ice cube
  • Ask prediction questions
  • Record student responses on board
Phase 2: Main Activity (25 minutes)

Students will observe as we heat the ice and record their observations at each stage of the process.

  1. Place ice in beaker
  2. Record initial temperature
  3. Apply heat gradually
  4. Record temperature every 2 minutes
  5. Note physical changes observed
Assessment

Students will complete an exit ticket identifying examples of solids, liquids, and gases from their daily life.
